--- layout: post title: Automatically save Foldings in vim tags: - linux - vim status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- Normaly you have to type allways :mkview and :loadview to save your folds, so simply add this two lines to your ~/.vimrc:
au BufWinLeave * silent! mkview au BufWinEnter * silent! loadview
Now, each time you close a file, its fold state will be saved and reloaded when you reopen the file in Vim.
