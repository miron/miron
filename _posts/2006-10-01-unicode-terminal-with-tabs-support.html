--- layout: post title: Unicode terminal with tabs support, clickable url's tags: - linux - terminal - unicode status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- After some time of search i found rxvt-unicode. With compiled in perl support you can start it with: urxvt -pe tabbed or add: URxvt.perl-ext-common: default,tabbed to your ~/.Xdefaults ^shift cursor-left or cursor-right switches tabs, ^shift cursor-down creates new tab. To leftclick an url in the terminal and open it for example in elinks, add: URxvt.urlLauncher: elinks URxvt.matcher.button: 1 Then start with urxvt -pe matcher or simply add: URxvt.perl-ext-common: matcher to your ~/.Xdefaults (you can combine it with the tabs entry). More useful hints: man urxvtperl
